# Costinvoice Integration with GitHub: A Step-by-Step Guide
Here is how to seamlessly integrate Costinvoice with GitHub to simplify your invoicing process and enhance project management. This guide will help you connect these two powerful tools, making your invoicing straightforward and efficient.

## Step 1: Set Up Your Costinvoice Account
- **Action:** Create an account on Costinvoice.
- **Why:** Access to invoicing features helps you manage client payments effectively.
- **Mini example:** Visit [Costinvoice](https://costinvoice.com), click on "Sign Up," fill in your details, and verify your email to activate your account.

## Step 2: Link Your GitHub Repository
- **Action:** Connect your GitHub account to Costinvoice.
- **Why:** Automating invoicing for projects hosted on GitHub saves time and reduces errors.
- **Mini example:** In your Costinvoice dashboard, go to "Integrations" and select GitHub. Authorize Costinvoice to access your GitHub account and link specific repositories.

## Step 3: Customize Your Invoices
- **Action:** Modify invoice templates for your GitHub projects.
- **Why:** Tailoring invoices to reflect your brand ensures a professional appearance that resonates with clients.
- **Mini example:** Navigate to "Templates" in Costinvoice, choose a template to edit, add your logo, adjust colors, and include project details relevant to your GitHub work.

## Step 4: Send Invoices Directly from GitHub
- **Action:** Use Costinvoice to send invoices.
- **Why:** Streamlining the payment process allows you to send invoices without leaving GitHub.
- **Mini example:** After completing a project, go to your linked GitHub repository and use the Costinvoice integration option to generate and send an invoice directly from there.

## Step 5: Track Payments and Manage Clients
- **Action:** Monitor payment statuses through Costinvoice.
- **Why:** Efficiently tracking finances ensures you know which invoices have been paid and which are outstanding.
- **Mini example:** Access the "Payments" section in your Costinvoice dashboard to view a summary of all transactions, filter by date, and check client payment statuses. By following these steps, you can effectively integrate Costinvoice with GitHub, simplifying your invoicing process and enhancing your professional image with clients.
- **Recap:**
- Set up your Costinvoice account to access invoicing features.
- Link your GitHub repositories to automate invoicing.
- Customize invoices to reflect your brand and project specifics. Next, consider exploring additional features in Costinvoice to further enhance your invoicing and project management capabilities.
